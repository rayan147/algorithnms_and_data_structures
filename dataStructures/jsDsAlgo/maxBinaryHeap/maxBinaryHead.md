## MaxBinaryHeap
 what is a MaxBinaryHeap?
 it is binary tree which parents are always greater than their children

 ![MaxBinaryHeap](images/maxBinaryHeap.png =250x)

### Big(0)
Big (0) | actions
--------| -------
O(log N)| insertion
O(log N)| removal
O(N)    | search

### Use Cases
  * sorting
  * priority queues
